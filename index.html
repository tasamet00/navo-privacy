Navo: Organize Your Life — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-29

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Navo (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses and discloses information when you use the mobile app Navo: Organize Your Life (the “App”).

2. Information We Collect

  • Local data: The app stores non-personal data locally on the device using PlayerPrefs and a local JSON file (tasks, app settings). This data stays on the user’s device.
  • Advertising & analytics: We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may process device identifiers and related ad signals. We do not collect or store personal user data on our servers.

3. How We Use Data

We use stored local data to display tasks, reminders, and app preferences. We use third-party services (AdMob) for monetization. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

4. Ads and Third-Party Services

We display ads through Google AdMob. Third-party ad providers may collect information to deliver personalized ads. For details on how AdMob collects and uses data, see Google’s privacy resources: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Subscription & Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are handled by Google Play Billing. Google manages the payment and related data. We do not receive your payment credentials.

6. Data Retention & Security

Local data is stored on the device. We implement reasonable safeguards but cannot guarantee absolute security. Do not store sensitive personal information inside the app.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy. We’ll post the new policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
